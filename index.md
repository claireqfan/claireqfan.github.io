<!-- ## About Me -->

Welcome! I am a postdoc in the [UBC Food and Resource Economics Group](https://fre-research.landfood.ubc.ca/). My research uses applied microeconomics to study the political economy of natural resource management, especially in developing countries. Please reach out if you'd like to discuss research!

I obtained my PhD from the University of Chicago Harris School of Public Policy. In summer 2027, I will join Lehigh University as an assistant professor in the [Dept. of Economics](https://business.lehigh.edu/departments/economics) and [ACES](https://aces.lehigh.edu/).
